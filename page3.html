Introduction


There is nothing more important and rewarding than food (Varona 1991). Food is one of the basic human needs. It is a source of energy that enables man to meet the basic drive for doing everything in life activities.

Human may consume a wide range of different food substances as long as they meet nutrition requirements. Others select and eat food that is influenced by other factors, such as their life style, psychological, physiological, economical, social and cultural needs. Others considered food of smell, taste, texture, and eye appeal. If an individual is knowledgeable of food nutrients, he/she will select food for its nutritional quality. Good health with good nutrition. People need differrent kinds of food in order to have a balance of nutrients that the body needs. Altogether they provide the body the needed substances to sustain life.

Sweet potato is rich not only in carbohydrates but also in vitamins and minerals. It is an excellent source of pro-vitamins (carotene), vitamin B complex in the form of riboflavin, niacin and thiamine are also present in the leaves of sweet potato.

Ipomea Batatas (Sweet Potato) or camote in our dialect, a family species of convolvulaceas is grown in tropical regions. It need four to five months of warm weather to mature. Since the Philippines is situated near the equator, it is not difficult to grow sweet potatoes.

Sweet Potatoes are also considered as a potential source of flour. Importation of flour would be considerably reduce if sweet potato flour substitute is available in commercial quantiities.


Statement of the Problem


The research is entitled "The Development of Sweet Potato Flour"

This study aims to determine the qualities of sweet potatoflour by answering the following questions:

1. What are the returned products of flour with one (1) kilogram of sweet potato root crops?

2. Is there significant difference of sweet potato flour to commercial flour in terms of:

2.1 price;

2.2 color;and

2.3 grain?

3. What are the sensory qualities of sweet potato flour in terms of:

3.1 color;and

3.2 grain


Significance of the Study


The significance of The study would encourage the researches to do more research on products using the sweet potato flour, Specifically, it is significant to:

Food Technology Teachers. The result of the study would be the bases for them to introduce similar products from sweet potatoflour that would give knowledge to the people.

Housewives. The result of the study would provide knowledge and awareness among housewives in using sweet potato flour. They can utilize sweet potatoflour in order to sustitute for expensive commercial flour.

Farmers. The result of the study would encourage them to plant more "camote" to answer the forseen increasing demand of sweet potato flour.

Community. This study would generate awareness to the residents to produce sweet potato thereby improving the family income as well as the nutritional status of the peopleespecially the children.

Students. The result of the study would be thebasis for them tointroduce similar products from sweet potato flour.
